FAQ

A copier lease is a contract that allows businesses to rent a copier or multifunction printer (MFP) for a set period, usually 12 to 60 months, instead of purchasing it outright. This option helps manage cash flow while gaining access to the latest office equipment.

Leasing a copier helps reduce upfront costs, provides predictable monthly payments, and gives you the flexibility to upgrade to newer models as technology advances. It's a cost-effective way to keep your office running efficiently.

Our copier lease packages typically include equipment, installation, maintenance, and technical support. We also offer optional service agreements that cover toner, parts, and repairs to minimize downtime.

Lease terms usually range from 12 to 60 months. At A1 Image Office Systems, we offer flexible lease options tailored to your business needs and budget.

At the end of your lease, you typically have several options: upgrade to a new copier, extend the current lease, purchase the equipment, or return it. We'll work with you to determine the best choice for your business.
